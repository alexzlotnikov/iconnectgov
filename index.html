Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

iConnectGov ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of information collected through our warrant resolution platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Law Enforcement Agency Information

  • Agency contact information
  • Officer credentials and system access logs
  • Warrant query data and resolution records
  • Vehicle and equipment identifiers

2.2 Court Information

  • Court system identifiers and jurisdiction data
  • Warrant information (case numbers, amounts, statuses)
  • Payment and resolution records
  • Integration API usage data

2.3 Defendant Information

  • Warrant details and amounts owed
  • Payment transaction data
  • QR code scan and resolution timestamps
  • Payment method information (processed by third-party payment providers)

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Facilitate real-time warrant queries and roadside resolution
  • Process payments and clear warrants across jurisdictions
  • Maintain system security and CJIS compliance
  • Generate analytics and reports for agencies and courts
  • Improve platform functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • FBI Criminal Justice Information Services (CJIS) compliance
  • Azure GovCloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication for system access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Role-based access controls and audit logging

Payment card data is handled exclusively by PCI-DSS compliant third-party payment processors and never stored in our systems.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Between Jurisdictions: Warrant data shared between originating courts and resolving agencies
  • Payment Processors: Transaction data shared with secure payment providers
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors supporting platform infrastructure (all under strict NDAs)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request

We do not sell personal information to third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain information according to:

  • CJIS data retention requirements
  • State and local record-keeping laws
  • Contractual obligations with agencies and courts
  • Minimum necessary periods for operational and legal purposes

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@iconnectgov.com

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify agencies and courts of material changes via email and system notifications.

10. Contact Us
